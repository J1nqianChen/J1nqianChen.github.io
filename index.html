About me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Xi’an Jiaotong University under the supervision of Prof. Jihua Zhu. I have also received the Bachelor degree of software engineering at Xi’an Jiaotong University in 2021.

My research area mainly focuses on machine learning, with special interests in federated learning, uncertainty estimation and point cloud processing.

Research Interests

  • Federated Learning
  • Uncertainty Estimation
  • Multimodal
  • Point Cloud
